AdoptCon

Hi! This is AdoptCon. Millions of animals are waiting in shelters for people to adopt them. Our job is raise awareness and convince people to adopt these precious animals. We bring many enthusiastic pet lovers together in one convention in order to bring attention to animal adoption. Many celebrities together who back our cause and often show up at our events, stengthening and furthering our message. Check us out, come to our events, adopt a pet!
